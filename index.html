The Anatomy of a
Plant Pot (The
Box Model)

Content: The Plant

The actual text or image you
want to show.

Padding: The Soil

The space inside the pot,
surrounding your plant.

Border: The Pot

The physical edge that holds
everything together.

Margin: The Gap

The space between different pots in your garden.
